📚 Cry Types Guide

😢

Hunger

Rhythmic, repetitive, low-pitched. Often with rooting or sucking motions.

😣

Pain/Colic

High-pitched, sudden onset, intense. May arch back or pull legs up.

😴

Sleepy/Tired

Whiny, intermittent, building slowly. Eye rubbing, yawning.

😖

Discomfort

Fussy, variable pitch. Often from wet diaper, temperature, or gas.

🤒

Illness

Weak, moaning, nasal quality. May have fever or lethargy signs.

👂

Ear Pain

Intense when lying down, pulling at ears. Often with cold symptoms.
